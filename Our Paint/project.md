# Our Paint

Our Paint is a painting program which has minimal feature sets yet achieves awesome result by having pigment-based color model and node-based brushes.

Hand made by Wu Yiming (https://ChengduLittleA.com)

### Further Links:
https://ChengduLittleA.com/OurPaint

## Slide 0 - What's special about Our Paint
![Title](./slide0.png)

- Pigment-based color model
- Node based brush system
- Infinite canvas

## Slide 1 - What's new
![slide 1](./slide1.png)

- The new pigment color model, mainly the [two component spectral pixel system](https://wellobserve.com/index.php?post=20250411214820).
- Accumulation and Depletion modeling, simulating the effect of paints leaving brushes and overlaying on the canvas.
- Using LZMA for internal compression makes the canvas file much smaller.
- Stability improvements for handling huge canvases.
- Soft proofing in color management.
- And many things...
